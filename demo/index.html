Governed Investigation — TLC Runtime Demo

A live demonstration of the Living Constitution (TLC) framework from the paper "Governed Investigation, Not Fluent Mirrors". Read this before you touch anything.

What is this?

Standard AI systems produce fluent, confident answers — then stop. They are fluent mirrors: they reflect your question back at you with statistical polish but no interpretive depth. When those systems are applied to Black consumer behavior, they routinely misclassify loyalty as inertia, betrayal responses as anomalies, and protective social strategies as suspicious behavior.

This demo shows a different approach: a governed investigator that cannot emit an output until it has checked eight structural conditions — the Eight Wonders of Black Shopping — against the incoming evidence. If any condition is uncertain or violated, the system halts and demands human clarification instead of guessing.

The Eight Wonders — what is the system actually checking?

I₁ Trust
Will this brand do right by me? Fails when a product is secretly reformulated without telling loyal customers.
I₂ Authenticity
Is this brand genuinely part of our world? Fails when brands dilute their identity or appropriate cultural symbols.
I₃ Status
What does this purchase say about me publicly? Fails when packaging is downgraded or the brand publicly disrespects its customers.
I₄ Identity Signaling
Does this product belong in my life and community? Fails when products mismatch cultural context.
I₅ Enacted Fidelity
I keep buying. Does that mean anything? Loyalty here is a covenant — not habit. It shatters on betrayal, not on a coupon.
I₆ Perceived Quality
Can this product handle my real life — not just a lab test? Fails when real-world use falls short of the brand promise.
I₇ Contextual Performance
Will it show up when it really matters — a cookout, a church event, a big occasion? Fails on scale and event failures.
I₈ Narrative
The upstream gate. What story does this community carry about this brand across generations? Every other Wonder depends on this one being intact. If Narrative is ambiguous, the whole system stops.

What do the colored states mean?

ACTIVE
All conditions checked and clear. Output is permitted.
HALTED
A condition was violated. Output is blocked. Human must repair before continuing.
AMBIGUOUS
Not enough data to decide. The system refuses to guess — it asks for more context.
REPAIR
A human submitted a repair action. System is re-evaluating.
INITIALIZING
Session just started. No telemetry received yet.
RESOLVED
Session completed cleanly with all conditions satisfied and output emitted.

How to use this demo — start here

1
Click + New Session on the left. This opens a Contract Window — a live runtime record the governed system maintains throughout a session.
2
Pick a scenario from the left panel. Each one is a real-world situation. Read the description before running it — it tells you what the AI will be fed and what should happen.
3
Watch the center panel. Each of the 8 Wonder cards will turn green (SATISFIED), red (VIOLATED), or amber (AMBIGUOUS). The FSM badge at the top tells you the overall system state.

What am I looking at?

Three panels: Left — session control and scenario launcher. Center — the live Contract Window showing all 8 invariants and the FSM state. Right — event log, human repair action, and truth ledger.

What is a "session"?

A session is one instance of the Contract Window — the runtime record the TLC system maintains while investigating a consumer behavior question. Create a new one for each experiment. You can run multiple scenarios in the same session to see how state accumulates.

Why does the system HALT?

When any of the 8 Wonders is VIOLATED (a condition is actively broken) or AMBIGUOUS (not enough data to decide), the system refuses to emit output. A fluent mirror would just produce an answer. The governed investigator stops and demands human input. That is the safety guarantee.

What is the Truth Ledger?

Every claim the system makes gets an epistemic tag: VERIFIED means it traces to confirmed data. CONSTRUCTED means the model inferred it — it might be wrong. PENDING means it is contested and needs review. If more than 3 CONSTRUCTED claims accumulate without a human sign-off, the system halts.

What is I₈ Narrative and why does it matter most?

Narrative is the upstream gate. It asks: does this system know the multigenerational story this community carries about this brand or institution? Without that context, every other Wonder is uninterpretable. It is listed last by number but evaluated first by the engine — if Narrative is AMBIGUOUS, the session immediately enters AMBIGUOUS state and no further evaluation proceeds.

What is the "Frontin'" scenario?

A Black shopper enters a heavily surveilled big-box store and deploys deliberate presentation management: precise grooming, visible premium brand markers, focused path movement. An ungoverned model reads the deliberateness and tension as suspicious. The governed investigator recognizes it as rational protective behavior under documented surveillance conditions and outputs NOMINAL.

Why does Euphemism Injection fail?

The Bicameral Review includes a pattern detector for corporate sanitization language — phrases like "geographic footprint optimization" that mask store closures in Black neighborhoods. Even if all 8 Wonder invariants pass, the Bicameral Review catches the language and blocks output.
